Our Mission

At anbessa Construction Company, our goal is to provide premier good quality concrete building materials at an affordable price for a wide range of construction needs, and we provide reliable service for our customers.

At our plant in Nduba Sector, we are capable of manufacturing large amount of custom-made building materials with different mix ratios for all kinds of building requirements.

Whether you're working on a residential, commercial, or industrial project, our blocks are designed to offer exceptional strength, durability, and versatility.
